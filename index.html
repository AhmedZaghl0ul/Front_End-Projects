ZAGHLOUL UNIVERSITY

The Higher Technological Institute is an Egyptian private higher institute, established by Dr. Mustafa Kamel in Egypt in 1988 in accordance with Law No. 52 of 1970. It offers three specializations: engineering, business administration, and computers and information. It is located in the cities of: 10th of Ramadan, 6th of October, and Marsa Matrouh.
